About Our Church

Our History

Founded in 1990, Akaki Brethren Church has been a beacon of hope and faith for the community. We started as a small gathering in a home and have grown to serve hundreds of families.

Our Mission

To spread the love of God, nurture spiritual growth, and serve our community with compassion and grace.
